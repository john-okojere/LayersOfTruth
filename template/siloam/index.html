Siloam is a 4 Day Conference

At Siloam, saints across board fellowship through soul enriching worship, prayers and interactions in an atmosphere of sincere love. There will be room for questions and answers where doctrinal issues will be appropriately addressed. Ministers will also be present as helpers of your Joy, who will bring hearty counsel to bear on issues bordering on your walk and work with God.
