SILOAM CONFERENCE 2023

Siloam is a non-denominational 4-day conference hosted annually by The Layers of Truth Church, Kaduna.
It is usually a time of in-depth study of God’s word, line upon line and precept upon precept, bringing great revelation light that leads to restoration, renewal and revival.

Siloam is a 4-Day Conference

At Siloam, saints across board fellowship through soul enriching worship, prayers and interactions in an atmosphere of sincere love. There will be room for questions and answers where doctrinal issues will be appropriately addressed. Ministers will also be present as helpers of your joy, who will bring hearty counsel to bear on issues bordering on your walk and work with God.

4-day Conference

7th - 10th April 2023

01

Open your eyes to see the light of the glorious gospel of Christ, and watch your life transform.

02

Delve into the waters of Siloam with eyes opened, and heart prepared to encounter God.

03

Set your gaze on the face of the Master, unfazed and listen for what he says.

04

Be made clean with the washing of water by the words he will be Speaking to you.

What To Expect In Siloam 2023 Conference

Siloam 2023 Conference offers all of the resources you need to experience God, You will encounter God in diverse ways and manners.
