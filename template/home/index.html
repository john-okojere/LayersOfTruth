WHAT WE BELIEVE


THE CHURCH


The church is the body of Christ, the habitation of God through the Spirit with divine appointments for the fulfillment of the great commission. Each believer, born of the Spirit, is an integral part of the church of the firstborn, which are written in heaven (Ephesians 1:22-23; 2:22; Hebrews 12:33).

THE GOD HEAD


The one true God who has revealed Himself by His Son Jesus Christ the mediator between God and mankind. God became manifest in the flesh for the redemption of mankind and righteouness for his people.Through his spirit. (Deuteronomy 6:4; Isaiah 43:10, 11; Matthew 28:19; Acts).
